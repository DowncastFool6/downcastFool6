London, United Kingdom

London is the third most visited city, welcoming 22.7 million international tourists. Famous for its cultural heritage, historical landmarks, and diverse neighborhoods, London offers attractions such as Buckingham Palace and the British Museum. Its rich arts scene, shopping districts, and parks make it a perennial favorite for travelers.
